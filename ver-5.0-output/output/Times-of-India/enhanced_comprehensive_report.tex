\section{Executive Summary}

This enhanced report presents a comprehensive feature-value analysis of linguistic differences between reduced register (newspaper headlines) and canonical forms. The analysis identified \textbf{30,847} difference events across \textbf{18} distinct linguistic features with \textbf{1,197} unique transformation types.

\subsection{Key Feature-Value Insights}
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Dependency Relation Change} (DEP-REL-CHG): 821 unique transformations, most frequent: \texttt{det→compound} (272 cases)
\item \textbf{Sentence Length Change} (LENGTH-CHG): 140 unique transformations, most frequent: \texttt{13→10} (39 cases)
\item \textbf{Surface Form Change} (FORM-CHG): 77 unique transformations, most frequent: \texttt{said→says} (4 cases)
\item \textbf{Dependency Head Change} (HEAD-CHG): 56 unique transformations, most frequent: \texttt{3→2} (58 cases)
\item \textbf{Lemma Change} (LEMMA-CHG): 20 unique transformations, most frequent: \texttt{United→Unite} (2 cases)
\end{itemize}
