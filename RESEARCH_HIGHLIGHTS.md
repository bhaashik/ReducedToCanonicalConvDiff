## üß† **Theoretical Contributions**

### **1. Register Reduction is Systematic, Not Random**
Predictable transformation hierarchies:
1. **Syntactic restructuring** (60.7% of changes)
2. **Lexical compression** (23.4% of changes)
3. **Structural modification** (12.1% of changes)
4. **Morphological preservation** (3.8% of changes)

### **2. Information Structure Drives Transformation**
- **Constituent movement** prioritizes news-worthy information
- **Dependency restructuring** emphasizes key relationships
- **Function word deletion** removes redundant grammatical markers

### **3. Functional vs. Content Word Treatment**
- **Function words**: Systematically deleted (85% article removal)
- **Content words**: Selectively modified (79% noun deletions vs varied additions)
- **Supports**: Functional load theory in register variation
